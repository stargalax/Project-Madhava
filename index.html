From Logic Gates to Silicon

Project Madhava is a hands-on hardware initiative based at IIT Gandhinagar, rebuilding the pathway from circuits to microcontrollers,empowering a new generation of engineers to not just use computers, but build them.

Join the Mission

Why India? Why now?

While most learn to program at high levels of abstraction, very few experience the complete journey from logic gates to working silicon.

As India invests in semiconductor manufacturing and design, the need for engineers who deeply understand computer architecture and hardware systems has never been greater.

Project Madhava seeks to bridge this gap through immersive, hands-on pedagogy that integrates theory with real fabrication.
